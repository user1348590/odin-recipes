Taco

This is a simple taco. For the sake of simplicity, I will only use a few ingredients. It only takes 15 or 20 minutes to make as many ingredients are just heated up.

Ingredients

  • Shells
  • Ground Beef
  • shredded Cheese
  • Any other toppings of your choice

Steps

  1. Heat up shells in the oven
  2. Cook Ground Beef
  3. Mix in seasoning packet while cooking on stove
  4. "construct" your taco and add whichever toppings you prefer
