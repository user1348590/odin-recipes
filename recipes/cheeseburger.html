cheeseburger

This is simply a classic American cheeseburger. This can be made in many different ways, but I am going to be making it on an outdoor grill. Just like the taco, toppings will vary depending on the person.

Ingredients

  • Sesame Bun
  • Ground Beef
  • Any sliced cheese
  • Seasoning of choice
  • Topping of choice

Steps

  1. After placing beef into a bowl, season to preference
  2. Form beef into patty
  3. Place patty on grill
  4. Flip when ready
  5. Place cheese on top while still on heat
  6. Once cooked to preference, construct burger
